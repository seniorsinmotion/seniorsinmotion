Citywide Prescription Delivery

If your patients are running low on time, and it's not convenient for them to wait for a prescription to be filled, take advantage of our citywide delivery service. We do what we can to help save you valuable time - simply drop off your patient's prescriptions. We work with your pharmacy staff to make all the arrangements for your patients.

Home Delivery Plan

Patients can order and receive your prescriptions in the comfort of their own home. We deliver their prescriptions the same day they are ready during our daily delivery rounds.
Any of our other products can be included with your prescription and will be delivered at the same time.
